Exclusive Bangkok Canal, Grand Palace & Reclining Buddha Private Day Tour

Highlights:

Cruise through the city's enchanting canals on a longtail boat, feeding the friendly fish along the way. Visit the magnificent Grand Palace, a dazzling showcase of Thai architecture and home to the sacred Emerald Buddha. Step into a world of tranquility at Wat Pho, where you'll marvel at the colossal Reclining Buddha and explore the temple's intricate details. Don't miss the chance to see the stunning Royal Barge Museum, showcasing the grandeur of Thailand's royal heritage. Our tour is perfect for families and anyone seeking an unforgettable experience.

Tour Itinerary

Morning: Royal Splendor and Canal Charm

08:30 AM: Pick-up at hotel
09:30 AM: Arrive at River City Pier and board a private longtail boat
09:30 - 10:30 AM: Enjoy a scenic canal tour, feeding the fish, and visiting the Royal Barge Museum
10:45 AM: Disembark and walk to the Grand Palace and Wat Phra Kaew
12:00 PM: Conclude Grand Palace visit

Afternoon: Cultural Immersion and Relaxation

12:15 PM: Lunch break (location to be determined) - Lunch is not included
13:15 PM: Visit Wat Pho, home to the Reclining Buddha
14:15 PM: Visit Wat Arun, Temple of Dawn - Landmark of Bangkok
15:15 PM: Tour concludes - Drop back at the hotel or we can drop you off at =>

Optional Evening Activities:

Chao Phraya River Cruise: Experience Bangkok's skyline from the water
Lumphini Park: Enjoy a leisurely stroll or jog in the city's green oasis
Icon Siam: Indulge in world-class shopping, dining, and entertainment.
Asiatique The Riverfront: Explore a vibrant night market with diverse shops, restaurants, and cultural performances.
Our Expert Guides will curate a perfect itinerary based on your interests, ensuring an unforgettable experience.
